sort1 uses: Bubble sort

How do you know?: Big Omega(n) is always fast, but time increases for each bigger unsorted set.

sort2 uses: Merge sort

How do you know?: Big O(n) = Big Omega(n). Is much faster than selection sort.

sort3 uses: Selection sort

How do you know?: It is the slowest on an already sorted set. Big Omega(n) is always n squared.
